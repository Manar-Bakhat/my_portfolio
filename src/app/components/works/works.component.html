NTT DATA Carrers

The project involved developing a web application for managing internship applications. Candidates can create their profile, upload a CV, and attach a cover letter. The application allows them to track their application's status and receive email notifications for any updates.

Results:

  • Optimization of the application process for both candidates and administrators.
  • Improved communication between candidates and the recruitment team through automated notifications.
  • Time-saving for administrators through centralized management of applications.
  • Protection of sensitive data through a robust authentication system.

LabGenius Project

I participated in the digitalization of the measurement testing process at TE Connectivity's laboratory. The goal was to automate the management of test requests and improve the traceability of operations. I contributed to the development of a web application to facilitate the management of tests and reports.

Results:

  • Automated test request management and reminders, reducing manual effort.
  • Improved test tracking and reporting efficiency, speeding up decision-making.
  • Enhanced traceability of test operations, improving quality control and compliance.
  • Achieved cost savings by eliminating paper records and manual processes.
